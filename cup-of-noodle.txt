Ingredients:
    -1 cup of noodles cup
    -1/2 cup of water

Directions :
 1. Microwave 1/2 cup of water until boiling hot
 2. Peel the top of cup of noodles half way back, remove seasoning package
 3. Add hot water and gourmet seasoning package to cup of noodles
 4. Cover and let stand for 2 minutes, mix and recover for 1 minute.
